# Lead Validation & Strategic Finance Dashboard 

An AI-driven analytics platform designed to reduce investment uncertainty in B2B Soft-Landing operations.

##  Business Strategy
This project solves the "high-uncertainty" problem in B2B market entry by:
- **Financial Stress-Testing:** Simulating 3 service tiers (Basic, Lite, Premium) to calculate ROI and Payback periods.
- **The "Semáforo" Logic:** A proprietary lead qualification system (Hot, Potential, Cold) based on operational intent.
- **Data-Driven Decision Making:** Transforming messy Excel leads into a prioritized Strategic Opportunity Matrix.

##  Tech Stack
- **Frontend:** Streamlit (Python-based SaaS UI)
- **Data Engine:** Pandas (ETL & Normalization)
- **Visualization:** Plotly (Strategic Matrix & Financial Projections)
- **Architecture:** Clean Separation of Concerns (UI, Business Logic, Data Layer).
